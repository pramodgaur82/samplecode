Program

Workshop

MEG UK 2018 begins with a one-day educational workshop on the 26th March. The workshop contains a range of talks introducing a variety of modern analysis techniques for MEG data. Speakers will introduce relevant concepts for beginners and provide practical advice for those looking to utilise the methods described. The workshop will be beneficial for those who work, or are planning to work, in MEG research and already have some familiarity with the core concepts in analysis and data collection.
